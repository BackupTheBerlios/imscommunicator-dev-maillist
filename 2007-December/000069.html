Hi,

> I am just testing a "transparent" B2BUA with OpenIMSCore using
> IMS-Communicator as clients. I am now stuck in the situation that the
> PRACK request is not responded by IMS-communicator when it is sent by the
> B2BUA.

We probably found the root source of the problem: The RAck header field
generated by the Application Server is bad.
Instead of "RAck: 1522280230 1 INVITE" the B2BUA generates "RAck:
15222802621 INVITE".

This of course does not match the previously sent reliable response and
therefore it is perfect behaviour of IMS-communicator to silently ignore the
bad PRACK request.
